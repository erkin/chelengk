Chelengk is a work-in-progress library for symbolic manipulation and analysis of Turkish classical music through the makam system.

It is named after the military decoration.


A note on spelling

Throughout this website, as well as within the software, I will be omitting circumflex accents from words regardless of how commonly they are written with them.
